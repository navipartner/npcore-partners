Welcome to the NaviPartner API documentation. Here you will find comprehensive information on how to use all of our APIs and webhooks, spanning across all of our proudcts.  

We are continuously expanding our APIs and webhooks to meet the needs of our customers, so please reach out to us if you feel anything is missing. 

If you want to explore our APIs in a tool like Postman, you can download our OpenAPI file here: 
[OpenAPI JSON](https://fernsdk.blob.core.windows.net/npcore/default/openapi-json/openapi.json)
